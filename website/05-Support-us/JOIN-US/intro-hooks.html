Donations

Donations allow you to directly support the Global Campus. Choose the way that best suits you: a general donation; a specific one to support scholarships, internships or fellowships; a legacy or gift in memoriam of your loved one.

Sponsorships

Our Sponsorships & Partnerships Programme provide global engagement to build communities involved in implementing the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) worldwide. We offer different types of initiatives whose goal is to establish long lasting cooperation between institutions.
