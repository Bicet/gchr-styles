Donations

Donations allows you to support directly our institution. You could choose the way that best suits you among the general, particular (destined to scholarships, internships or fellowships) and the legacy in memoriam of your loved one.

Sponsorships

Our Sponsorships [&] Partnerships programmes are oriented to provide global engagement in order to build communities devoted to support the implementation of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) worldwide. They are of different types suited to establish a long lasting cooperation between institutions.
