This document was adopted in February 2017 and articulates our willingness to act and react to the increasing number of global crises and human rights challenges.

At a time when some scholars and politicians speak about the end of the era of human rights, the GC worldwide network has an important contribution to make in terms of strengthening universal values of human rights, the rule of law, and democracy.

Statute

[nbsp]

[nbsp]
[nbsp]

It identifies the nature and aims of the organisation, its institutional structure and membership, and the composition and competence of its governing bodies. The GC Statute evolved from the Statute of EIUC, the predecessor of the Global Campus, established in 2002 at the initiative of the European Union.

In 2017 the organisation revised it in order to reflect the global nature of its composition and governance.
