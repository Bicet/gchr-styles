Finance Department

We supervise Global Campus finances and liaise with GC partners in implementing the budget of the action grant. We monitor expenditure progress, supervise accounting practices by GC partners and HQ, take care of budgeting and financial reporting, prepare for audits and manage daily book-keeping, payments and other administrative tasks.

Finance Manager

Luca Fantinel

Luca has worked for the Global Campus since 2004. His main responsibility is to manage Global Campus resources. He supervises joint budgets and forecasts and consolidates the results of all partners' reports. He also draws up budgets and reports for new projects.

Luca holds a degree in economics from the Università Cattolica del Sacro Cuore in Milan and a Master in Business and Administration from the University of Kansas. He gathered experience in managing companies and developing startups.

Currently he also serves as Premises and Technical Services Manager.
