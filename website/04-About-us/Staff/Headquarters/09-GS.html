General Services Department

We take care of the organisation of travel, accommodation, catering services and local transportation for all Global Campus activities and events held in Venice. We also support travel and in some cases accommodation for most of the global activities managed by the HQ. We purchase any goods and services necessary for the daily activities of the headquarters staff.

General Services Manager

Alessandra Silanos

Alessandra actively contributes to the organization by supervising and coordinating the acquisition of goods and services. She also selects and deals with the providers, including negotiating rates and conditions.

Alessandra started working for the Global Campus in 2003 as the Logistics Officer. Over the years her responsibilities and tasks have changed and increased consistently with the development of the organisation.

Before joining the Global Campus she worked as an external consultant for the Office for the Protection of Architectural, Natural, Historic, Artistic and Ethno-Anthropological Heritage in Venice and the Lagoon (Soprintendenza ai Beni Architettonici e Paesaggistici di Venezia e Laguna).
