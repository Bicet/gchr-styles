Online Programmes Manager/Head of Department

Angela Melchiorre

Dr. Angela Melchiorre designs and manages all Global Campus e-learning activities. She has worked in human rights since 2000 with multi-sectoral experiences.

In her previous roles, she was Programme Director of the European Master's Degree in Human Rights and Democratisation (EMA) and Lecturer at the University of London where she earned her PhD with a thesis on child marriage and the Convention on the Rights of the Child. Other teaching activities include post-graduate courses in universities around Europe.

Dr. Melchiorre has also experience with ActionAid International, the Italian and EU delegations at the UN in Geneva and New York, and independent consultancies with UNESCO, UNICEF, UN CEDAW, the UN Special Rapporteur on the Right to Education.

Her areas of expertise are children's rights, the right to education and Human Rights in International Politics. She graduated from EMA in 2000.

Project Manager - Development of HR Education and Research at UNTL

Adriano Remiddi

Adriano is an experienced practitioner in the field of international cooperation for higher education. He is committed to promoting democracy and HR education in post-conflict, developing and transitional societies.

In the course of ten years spent in Southeast Europe and later Asia-Pacific, he has developed a distinctive mix of academic and managerial skills and gained a diversified and progressively responsible professional experience.

Since 2019 Adriano has led an EU-funded capacity building project aimed at establishing the Human Rights Centre of the National University of Timor-Leste (UNTL).

Previously he worked as Academic Tutor and subsequently Coordinator of the GC South East Europe/ERMA on behalf of the Institute for Central-Eastern and Balkan Europe/University of Bologna (IECOB).

As IECOB research fellow in regional studies he also lectured internationally, published, organised scientific conferences and participated in panels as speaker. Since 2014 he has been president of the alumni association of the MA in Eastern European Studies of the University of Bologna.
