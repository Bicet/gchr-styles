E-learning Department

We create and manage all distance-learning activities, including Massive Open Online Courses (MOOCs), specialised online courses and e-learning partnerships with key stakeholders. We also support and coordinate efforts in all GC Regional Programmes to complement on-site activities with[nbsp]online components (blended teaching).

Online Programmes Manager

Angela Melchiorre

Dr. Angela Melchiorre designs and manages all Global Campus e-learning activities. She has worked in human rights since 2000 with multi-sectoral experiences.

In her previous roles, she was Programme Director of the European Master's Degree in Human Rights and Democratisation (EMA) and Lecturer at the University of London where she earned her PhD with a thesis on child marriage and the Convention on the Rights of the Child. Other teaching activities include post-graduate courses in universities around Europe.

Dr. Melchiorre has also experience with ActionAid International, the Italian and EU delegations at the UN in Geneva and New York, and independent consultancies with UNESCO, UNICEF, UN CEDAW, the UN Special Rapporteur on the Right to Education.

Her areas of expertise are children's rights, the right to education and Human Rights in International Politics. She graduated from EMA in 2000.

Children's Rights E-learning Expert

Manuela Pegoraro

Manuela started working for the Global Campus in 2019. She has 15+ years’ experience in education, communication and project-management. She previously worked for an agency of the European Commission and in academic and non-profit organisations in Europe, the USA and the Middle East.

Her most enjoyable and successful experiences are related to the coordination of European networks and the management of multinational project teams. She currently contributes to the implementation of online educational programmes on children’s rights.

Manuela holds an MA in E-learning from the University of Florence and one in Cultural Management from the University of Northumbria at Newcastle.
