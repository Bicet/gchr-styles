Cancellation and Refund Policies

Cancellation by applicant:

  • Summer School registrations will not be confirmed until registration is complete and billing information is received in full
  • Cancellations received before 31 July 2020 will be fully reimbursed minus 50,00 € of administrative fees
  • Cancellations received after 1 August 2020 will be charged 50% of the total fee. Alternatively, the enrollment can be transferred to another Venice based programme or to the 2021 edition of CHRA (fees may have to be compensated if not similar)

Cancellation by Global Campus of Human Rights:

  • Global Campus of Human Rights is not responsible for any extra expenses (i.e. travel costs, Visa, etc.) incurred by the participant if a course is cancelled.
  • If the course is cancelled due to any unforeseen circumstances such as weather or natural disaster, the participant is entitled to reschedule for a future session of the course.
  • Global Campus of Human Rights reserves the right to cancel any course due to insufficient number of applications up to the deadline for enrolment. Notice will be provided with the full refund of registration fees minus 50,00 € of administrative fees.
