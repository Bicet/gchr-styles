Former Staff Members

Honorary President

Prof. Dr. Zdravko Grebo

In memoriam, 1947-2019

Co-founder of the ERMA Programme, Co-director until 2015 and subsequantly Honorary President, late Zdravko Grebo was Emeritus Professor of Law at the University of Sarajevo.

He has been the director of the Centre for Interdisciplinary Postgraduate Studies since its establishment until 2007, and a laureate of many academic awards and international distinctions.

Former Programme Co-Director

Prof. Stefano Bianchini

Co-founder of the ERMA Program and its Co-director until 2018, he is a full professor of East European History and Politics at the School of Political Science of the University of Bologna, Forlì Campus, and - since 1993 - has been coordinating the "Institute for Central-Easter and Balkan Europe".

Former Vice-Director

Prof. Francesco Privitera

ERMA Coordinator until 2016 and later Programme Vice-Director until 2018 on behalf of the University of Bologna unitil 2018, Francesco Privitera is Professor of Politics and History of Eastern Europe at university of Bologna and president of the the Institute for Central-Eastern and Balkan Europe.

Former Coordinator of the Academic and Development Unit

Adriano Remiddi

AFormer ERMA Coordinator of the Academic and Development Unit (2016-19) and Academic Tutor (2013-16) on behalf of the IECOB-University of Bologna, Adriano Remiddi has also worked as IECOB research associate, and assistant to the director at the CCSDD Johns Hopkins University SAIS Europe. After ERMA he joined the Global Campus of Human Rights as project manager.

Former Administrative Assistant

Maida Omanović

Maida Omanovic is the coordinator of the Master program European Studies and the Master program Management of State and NGOs at the U. of Sarajevo/CIS. She worked administrative assistant at the ERMA programme until 2018. She holds a master degree in Quality Management – a joint master program of the School of Economics and Business of the University of Sarajevo and the Faculty of Economics of the University of Zagreb.
