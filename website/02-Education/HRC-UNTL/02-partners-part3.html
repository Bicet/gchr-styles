National University of Timor-Leste (UNTL)

Since the UNTL is the final beneficiary institution, all main aspects of the project are coordinated with the UNTL Rector and his Cabinet, as well as with the Deans of the Faculties involved, especially the Faculty of Social Sciences which houses the Human Rights Centre.

Website: http://untl.edu.tl/pt/

Mahidol University (MU)

All academic aspects of capacity-building are conceptualized and developed thanks to the expert support of the Institute of Human Rights and Peace (IHRP) of Mahidol University in Bangkok, home to the Global Campus Asia-Pacific Programme. IHRP lecturers are involved in teaching and tutoring students and trainees, as well as supporting internationalization of the new HRC and facilitating its introduction to the main regional human rights education networks.

Website: https://op.mahidol.ac.th/ihrp/en/

Provedoria for Human Rights and Justice (PDHJ)

The Provedoria, as the main national human rights institution, is a crucial local actor. Their expertise makes a vital contribution to incorporating the Timorese experience in struggles for human rights and democratization into the training programmes and for curriculum development.

Website: http://pdhj.tl/?lang=en

Centro Nacional Chega! (CNC)

The Centro Nacional Chega, as the national centre for dealing with the past, is a crucial local actor. Their expertise makes a vital contribution to incorporating the Timorese experience in struggles for human rights and democratization into the training programmes and for curriculum development.

Website: https://www.centrochega.tl/en

United Nations in Timor-Leste

The UN system in Timor-Leste, including the Resident Coordinator Office and the Agencies, has strongly supported the project from its very inception. Their help is critical to further improve the implementation process, facilitate linkages with domestic and foreign stakeholders and support the organization of local events and future development of the Human Rights Centre.

Website: https://www.un.org.tl/en/
