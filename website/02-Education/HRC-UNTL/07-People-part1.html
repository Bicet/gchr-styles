Project Team

Project Manager

Adriano Remiddi

Former coordinator of the GC South East Europe Programme for the University of Bologna in Sarajevo, Adriano is in charge of the overall designing and implementation of the project, with the support of key offices and colleagues of the GC headquarters in Venice, the GC Asia-Pacific regional hub, and the project team in Dili.

For information: adriano.remiddi@gchumanrights.org

Project Officer

Dr. Li Li Chen

Ph.D in Political Science at the University of Florida and visiting Professor at the UNTL in Dili, Li Li supports the organization of the academic operations of the project, especially its extra-curricular activities and the creation of the Centre’s library. Li Li also helps the project to liaise with UNTL bodies and other local civil society partners.

Project Officer

.Coordinator at the Henrich Boll Stiftung – Sarajevo Office, and an alumna of the Global Campus, between July 2019 and September 2020, Jelena supported the project cycle management, as well as the development of training programmes and study materials. Project Assistant

GC Asia-Pacific Programme Director

A senior human rights scholar at the IHRP-MU Institute of Human Rights and Peace Studies, Mahidol University (Bangkok), Mike acts as the main academic and implementation advisor to the project manager, as well as the focal point for the integration of the future UNTL-HRC within the GC Asia-Pacific network.
