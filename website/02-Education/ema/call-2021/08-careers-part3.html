GC EU-UN Fellowship Programme

The EU-UN Fellowship Programme complements the training activities of EMA by providing high-level operational working experience for four EMA graduates each year. This unique programme was initiated in 1996 by the Human Rights and Democratisation Unit of the European Commission and has since gained an excellent reputation and appreciation for the assistance that the fellows provide and the exceptional experience that the fellows gain.


As the fellows are assigned to the EU Delegations to the United Nations (Geneva and New York), the programme provides EMA graduates with an opportunity to gain advanced insight into EU activities at the UN, thus creating a multiplier effect for future work experiences. At the same time, it boosts the human resources capacity of EU offices by providing them with young professionals who possess specific expertise in the promotion of human rights.

The fellows become an integral part of the delegation, acting (under due supervision) as human rights experts, providing helpful support and information on human rights issues, and gaining extensive insight into diplomatic activities.
