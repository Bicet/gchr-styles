FRAME

FRAME was a large-scale, collaborative research project funded under the EU’s Seventh Framework Programme (FP7). It was coordinated by the Leuven Centre for Global Governance Studies and conducted by 19 research institutes from around the world, including 15 Global Campus participating universities. Research focused on the contribution of the EU’s internal and external policies to the promotion of human rights worldwide.

The FRAME project ran from 1 May 2013 to 30 April 2017.
