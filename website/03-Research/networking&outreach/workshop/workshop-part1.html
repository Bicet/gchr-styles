The development of workshops on integrated teaching involves the close collaboration between lecturers from two or more regional programmes at all stages of the process - from intellectual exchange in conceptualising the objectives and content of lectures and other activities, to joint delivery of teaching and public events and joint facilitation of discussion with the students.

This experience represents a further development of the exchange of professors and it has proven to be enriching both for the participating lecturers and for the students of the involved programmes.
