The Nelson Mandela World Human Rights Moot Court Competition in Geneva is organised in the framework of the Global Campus of Human Rights Master's Programmes under the leadership of the University of Pretoria.

This human rights educational event is unique in bringing together some of the youngest and brightest law students from universities all around the globe to debate burning contemporary human rights issues on the basis of a common UN human rights system, influenced by national and regional perspectives and experiences.

Following the UN model of representation all the regions of the world are represented. The idea is to create and support a worldwide platform where law students can debate and discuss Human Rights issues and development.
