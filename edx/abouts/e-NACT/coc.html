We trust you and count on the fact that you will not provide false information about yourself or allow somebody else to participate in your place. In the same vein, we expect that you:

  • do not reproduce content from the course or other students unless allowed by the express copyright terms of the course (e.g. Creative Commons);
  • do not share the solutions to quizzes with others;
  • do not submit the work of others as your own work.

This is an educational course based on open but always respectful and appropriate exchanges, so do not post content that:

  • insults, harasses, harms or threatens others;
  • violates copyright or intellectual property;
  • contains obscene material;
  • encourages illegal activities;
  • advertises or sells a product or service.

We also expect that you respect:

  • the diversity of opinions and cultures that will be present in your course;
  • the privacy of other students and instructors.

We do our best to monitor all activity and content in the course and reserve the right to remove inappropriate content and ban users who violate this Code of Conduct.

If you believe that someone has violated this code of conduct, please notify the coordinating team of the course at enact.moocs@gchumanrights.org.
